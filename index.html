Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, is one of the most famous monuments in the world. It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. This white marble mausoleum is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The Taj Mahal is known for its stunning architecture and intricate carvings. The beauty of the Taj Mahal is enhanced by the reflection in the Yamuna River. Every year, millions of tourists visit to witness this masterpiece of love.

Features of Taj Mahal

  • Built entirely of white marble.
  • One of the Seven Wonders of the World.
  • Symmetrical architecture with a central dome.
  • Beautiful gardens with fountains.
  • Calligraphy inscriptions from the Quran.
